🚀 About Me

I’m Trisha, a 2nd-year Computer Engineering (BSCpE) student at PUP – Sta. Maria. I exist at the intersection of aesthetic design and technical execution. From the physical texture of a canvas to the complex logic of software, I am driven by the challenge of bringing ideas to life.

I am a quiet coder who deeply cares about the harmony between functionality and aesthetics. I believe software should be as intuitive as it is beautiful. By applying my artistic intuition to system architecture, I build projects that are both efficient and imaginative.

In the world of art, there is no such thing as a "perfect" piece—but there is always a masterpiece. I aim for that level of excellence in every line of code I write and every system I design. ✨
